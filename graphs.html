Graphs and analytic combinatorics.

The question here is: can we apply the methods of analytic combinatorics to graphs? Thanks to the tools developed (among others) by Elie de Panafieu, we have rediscovered some results on the occurrence of small subgraphs, and obtained some new results.

List of publications

  • E. de Panafieu, G. Collet, D. Gardy, B. Gittenberger, V. Ravelomanana. Threshold functions for small subgraphs: an analytic approach. Eurocomb'17, August 2017, Wien (Austria). ArXiV extended version.
